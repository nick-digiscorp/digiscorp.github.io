ABOUT US

Digital Scorpion Interactive is an Indie Game Development Studio founded in 2021 by Aric Hood, and Nicholas Revell. They had a vision to change the way games are built and prove to the gaming world they are a force to be reckoned with. DSI was founded on the principles of Ingenuity, Integrity, and Innovation. They strive to break the boundaries of modern gaming and bring to life an immersive experience gamers will keep coming back for.

DSI is developing its premier game Hit N' Bit, a 2.5D Metroidvania. It currently does not have a set release date, but keep checking back for more updates!

HIT N' BIT - IN DEVELOPMENT

Metroidvania | PC

Players will experience the story of Hit N’ Bit through the sibling pair, Hitendra and Thybit. As Hitendra, the determined warrior princess, players will search all over Illumnia for her missing brother. Wielding a mighty sword and a heart for battle, she’ll let nothing stop her in her quest.
As Thybit, a master of the arcane arts, players will find themselves exploring the unknown realm of Tenebrye. Determined as ever, Thybit will track down the mysterious mage who brought him to the realm of darkness and stole his legendary staff of balance. Read More

HIT N' BIT

TRAVEL TO NEW LANDS

Hit N' Bit takes place in the wolrd of Valoris. A magical land, ruled by Old Gods and New. You will travel to new lands throughout the game, exploring the history and magic of the realms.


WELCOME TO CERULIS

Ceruils is the shining jewel in the sky. A beacon of all things good in the Realm of Illumnia. This is where the story begins with Hit N' Bit. Magic is strong, and the people are nice here.


ENTER MURWELL

The darker half of Valoris, Murwell the Sinking City of Tenebrye. You will explore the deep dark magic while attempting to escape its grasp on you. Will you get out alive?

CINEMATIC DESIGNER

DSI is looking for a talented and experienced Cinematic Designer to join our team.

CINEMTATIC DESIGNER

THE IDEAL CANDIDATE:

The ideal candidate should have a very good understanding of directing, storytelling, acting and cinematography as well as a strong sense of composition and pacing.

  • Storytelling
  • Unity
  • Cinematography
  • Blender
  • Composition & Pacing

JOB DESCRIPTION:

  • Creating compelling cutscenes with excellent pacing, cinematography, editing and timing.

  • Communicating with Designers and Leads to improve readability of visual storytelling and establish perfect continuity of the story with gameplay.


REQUIREMENTS:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Arts (preferably Animation / Film Production) and/or comparable years of professional experience in games/movies.

  • Experience with 3D software (Motionbuilder, 3DSMax, Maya, Unity, etc.)

  • Experience in using game engines: Unity (preferably), Unreal, Godot etc.

  • Strong communication and organizational skills

  • Must work well under pressure and handle multiple tasks

  • Passion for making GREAT games

  • Good knowledge of cinematography and editing.

  • Game development experience (doing a long-form project(s) from inception to completion).

VISUAL EFFECTS ARTIST

DSI is looking for a creative, detail-oriented individual to join our team as a visual effects artist.

VISUAL EFFECTS ARTIST

THE IDEAL CANDIDATE:

To be a successful visual effects artist, you should be highly creative with a strong working knowledge of standard industry software tools. Ultimately, a top-notch visual effects artist follows instructions given by supervisors and clients and is able to translate those instructions into amazing visual effects.

  • Unity
  • Blender
  • Creativity

JOB DESCRIPTION:

  • Creating visual effects and animations using computerized technology and software applications.

  • Producing visuals for video games and mobile applications.

  • Creating simulations and mockups to be viewed and approved by those involved in media production, including directors, engineers, and editors.

  • Troubleshooting any issues with animations.

  • Creating storyboards to understand the images needed for the storyline and where the images should be used.

  • Adhering to tight deadlines and other constraints in order to meet production times.

  • Brainstorming with artists and designers to discuss digital animation concepts, gaming ideas, and progress on current projects.

  • Report to Team Lead for updates or changes.


REQUIREMENTS:

  • Bachelor's or associate's degree in computer animation, multimedia studies, graphic design, or a related field.

  • Strong working knowledge of software tools, including Houdini, Unity, Dreamweaver, Adobe CS5, and Autodesk Maya.

  • Excellent computer skills and attention to detail.

  • Good drawing skills and creativity.

  • Excellent time management skills.

  • Development experience in the games industry a plus.

  • Strong communication and organizational skills

  • Must work well under pressure and handle multiple tasks

  • Passion for making GREAT games
