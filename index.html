Hit N' Bit

Hit N’ Bit is a dark fantasy Metroidvania platformer. Embark on two wildly different journeys where you will fight monsters, explore a story-rich world, and encounter challenging puzzles. Do you have what it takes to defeat evil and change your destiny?

Wishlist Now Play the Demo Join Our Discord

About Hit N' Bit

Hit N’ Bit is a 2.5D action-adventure metroidvania game that also holds a narratively-rich world. As Hitendra, you’ll encounter a plethora of non-player characters each with their own unique personalities. Talking to these people, even for a brief moment, will reveal insight about the world of Valoris. Hitendra uses this knowledge and her athletic ability to reach new locations to explore hidden areas and complete quests. Fight back those who threaten Hitendra and her city’s safety with her martial prowess.


The Story of Cerulis

The land of Valoris is one filled with magic and danger. Cerulis is the shining capital in the sky where many people call home. In this adventure you play as Hitendra, a warrior princess, as she prepares to set out on a journey to find her missing brother, Thybit. Explore the Cerulis Town Square in search of clues as well as gather the tools and supplies to make the journey as safe as possible. During your time preparing for adventure, you’ll also be assisting citizens and addressing their troubles. Dastardly figures and monsters run amok in the town square and it’s up to Hitendra’s amazing skills to bring back peace and justice to her home!

Tales of Valoris: Swallow's Defenders

Swallow’s Keep is a place of great prosperity; however, not all lands share in its wealth, and it has caught the eye of ambitious adversaries. Can you defend your home against the bandits?

Purchase Now Join Our Discord

40 High Stakes Levels

Many people across Illumnia require the aid of Swallow’s Keep. Survive this Tale of Valoris by completing 20 captivating levels across 4 chapters. The story begins with you defending your home from a bandit siege. After successfully protecting their home, players will have the opportunity to travel across the land to places such as the Oreliana Ruins to stop those that threaten their livelihood.


Command a Powerful Legion

The Green Prince’s army has a variety of ways to defend itself: unique human troops like the powerful Crossbow Women destroy enemies from afar and combat items like the Bear Trap shut down close-range attackers.


Perilous Opponents

From vaulting thieves to mad poets, many types of bandits seek the fortune of Swallow’s Keep. Be wary of the various enemies throughout each chapter, as each comes with its own boss you must overcome.


Strategy Wins the Day

Though the riches of Swallow’s Keep are vast, they are not endless. Be mindful of how you manage your resources and soldiers; an unprepared General is a fallen one.
