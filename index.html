ABOUT US

Digital Scorpion Interactive is an Indie Game Development Studio founded in 2021 by Nicholas Revell. He had a vision to change the way games are built and prove to the gaming world they are a force to be reckoned with. DSI was founded on the principles of Ingenuity, Integrity, and Innovation. They strive to break the boundaries of modern gaming and bring to life an immersive experience gamers will keep coming back for.

HIT N' BIT - IN DEVELOPMENT

Metroidvania | PC

Players will experience the story of Hit N’ Bit through the sibling pair, Hitendra and Thybit. As Hitendra, the determined warrior princess, players will search all over Illumnia for her missing brother. Wielding a mighty sword and a heart for battle, she’ll let nothing stop her in her quest.
As Thybit, a master of the arcane arts, players will find themselves exploring the unknown realm of Tenebrye. Determined as ever, Thybit will track down the mysterious mage who brought him to the realm of darkness and stole his legendary staff of balance. Read More

HIT N' BIT

TRAVEL TO NEW LANDS

Hit N' Bit takes place in the world of Valoris. A magical land, ruled by Old Gods and New. You will travel to new lands throughout the game, exploring the history and magic of the realms.


WELCOME TO CERULIS

Ceruils is the shining jewel in the sky. A beacon of all things good in the Realm of Illumnia. This is where the story begins with Hit N' Bit. Magic is strong, and the people are nice here.


ENTER MURWELL

The darker half of Valoris, Murwell the Sinking City of Tenebrye. You will explore the deep dark magic while attempting to escape its grasp on you. Will you get out alive?

TALES OF VALORIS: SWALLOW'S DEFENDERS

Tower Defense | Mobile

Prepare to defend Swallows Keep as mobs of thieves, bandits, and monsters threaten the citizens of our grand city. Players will command a variety of defenders to halt these evildoers in their tracks. Use your arsenal of skilled fighters and mages to creatively defeat your foes and stop the heinous plans of the coalition of mysterious bandit commanders. Read More

TALES OF VALORIS

A BLAST TO THE PAST

Set 50 years in the past within the Hit N’ Bit universe, you will experience a story of victory and loss as you follow along the tale of Halvesh and his band of heroes, who aim to keep their home safe from invaders.


A SERIES OF FUN AND CHALLENGING LEVELS

Conquer 20 levels that will take you through the land of Swallows Keep and the surrounding area. Experience the lush landscapes as you trek through dangerous territory to stop the onslaught of enemies in your path.


COMMAND A DIVERSE CAST OF TROOPS

As a commander of Swallows Keep, you will have the ability to command five defender types at a time to creatively defeat the thieves and bandits in your way. Earn 6+ different types of Defenders on your journey throughout Valoris and become stronger in your fight against your foes.
